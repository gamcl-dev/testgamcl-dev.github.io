Jellyflug

Welcome to the bacterial world of Jellyflug! Play as Grash the bacterium on his quest home to the bathroom. Meet many trials along the way as every day household rooms become enormous microbial worlds. Explore over 15 levels and meet over 45 characters in this platforming adventure. Jellyflug is available on iTunes, Google Play, Amazon and Samsung.
