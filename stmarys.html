St Marys App
School app to showcase the schools circulum and activies

The St. Mary's College mobile app puts all the latest news, information and interactivity at your fingertips. Now you can stay up to date with school events, keep important information handy, or just spot some familiar faces in the photo gallery. Developed on behalf of St. Mary's College Derry, this app offers a useful tool for parents and students alike to connect to their local school.
